Questions

Got Questions?

How quickly will I see results?
Most clients notice measurable changes within 3–4 weeks. Significant body composition shifts typically happen at the 8–12 week mark. Sustainable, permanent results take 3–6 months — and that's exactly what we build for.
Do I need a gym membership?
Not necessarily. I offer home workout programs with minimal equipment that deliver serious results. That said, gym access opens up more training possibilities, especially for the Elite and Champion plans.
What makes your coaching different?
Every program is built from scratch for you — not pulled from a template library. I combine exercise science with behavioural psychology, accountability systems, and lifestyle integration to get results that actually last.
Can I cancel my plan anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no lock-in contracts. I'm confident the results will keep you — not the fine print.
How does online coaching work?
You get access to my coaching app where your weekly program lives. We check in via video call weekly, and you have direct WhatsApp/in-app messaging access 7 days a week. It's as close to in-person coaching as you can get remotely.
Do you offer nutrition coaching separately?
Yes — standalone nutrition coaching is available. However, the combination of training + nutrition always yields the best results, which is why it's included in all plans.
